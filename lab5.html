In the second dataset of higgs and QCD, we have following features:

pt: transverse momentum eta: pseudorapidity phi: azimuthal angle mass: mass of the particle ee2: 2-point ECF ratio ee3 3-point ECF ratio d2: 3 to 2 point ECF ratio angularity: orientation of one feature to another at a referenced angle t1: 1-subjettiness t2: 2-subjettiness t3: 3-subjettiness t21: 21-subjettiness t32: 32-subjettiness

According to the introduction in the research report, the ratio of 2-point and 3-point generalized energy correlation functions can describe a distinct discrimination between signals as higgs and background as QCD.
